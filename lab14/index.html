Lab 14 - Debugging & Strategies

Challenge

The challenge of this lab was to go back to one of our old labs and to debug them! For the sake of this lab we used our Lab 8, Anonymous Functions & Callbacks.

Problems

The problem/bugs that occurred was that our arrays were not printing with correct commas and spaces. After feed back from one of our TA's(Ilia the GOAT!) we were able to figure out how to separate the arrays correctly using .join(", "). We also separated our outputs in different divs instead of putting them all into one.
